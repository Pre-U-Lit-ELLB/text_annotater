Texts
About this tool

Each annotator lets you highlight words and phrases by category (Imagery, Sound, Tone, Structure, Theme), view a running highlights log, take notes in a side panel, and access a glossary of context-specific terms — with an option to auto-generate one via AI.

Use Focus mode to isolate individual lines as you work through the text. Your highlights and notes are not saved between sessions.
